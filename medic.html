Rofenac-d 50mg tab

A drug that contains sodium diclofenac belongs to a group of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs as it works by blocking the effect of cyclo-oxygenase enzymes cyclooxygenase. These enzymes help to make other chemicals in the body called prostaglandins that are secreted at the sites of injury or damage and cause pain and inflammation.
